Adding Contacts

Tap the pencil icon or use the + button to add a contact. Enter their full email address to find them.

Voice Messages

Tap the microphone button to start recording. Tap again or press Send to send the message.

Groups & Broadcasts

Go to Menu → New Group or New Broadcast to create a group chat or send to multiple contacts.

Blocking Users

Open a chat, tap ⋮ menu → Block to block a user. Manage blocked users in Menu → Blocked Users.

QR Code

Share your QR code with friends to let them add you instantly. Tap the QR icon in the sidebar header.
